*Tyrannosaurus* is the **most** fierce dinosaur that ever exixted.

The T-Rex lived during the [Late Cretaceous Period](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Late_Cretaceous).

## Features

- Sharp Teeth
- Small Arms
- Thick Tail
